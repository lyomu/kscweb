Product details

Kazuri Bean is a premium variety known for its resilience, high yield, and rich nutritional content. Ideal for both small-scale and commercial farming, Kazuri beans thrive in diverse climatic conditions, ensuring consistent performance season after season. Features: High Yield: Expect a bountiful harvest with Kazuri's robust growth, which offers superior productivity per acre. Resilient: Engineered to withstand common bean diseases and pests, ensuring a healthy crop with minimal intervention. Nutrient-Rich: Packed with essential vitamins and minerals, Kazuri beans are a nutritious choice for a healthy diet. Versatile: Perfect for a variety of dishes, from traditional stews to modern culinary creations. Planting Instructions: Plant Kazuri beans in well-drained soil with adequate sunlight. Space seeds 2-3 inches apart and water regularly to maintain soil moisture. Harvest when the pods are full and dry for the best quality beans. Ideal For:Farmers looking to maximize their yield and quality, whether for personal use or market sale.
